About the System

Purpose and Design

This system is designed to support early screening for possible indicators associated with Type 1 Diabetes. By analyzing basic health measurements, symptoms, and selected laboratory indicators, the platform helps identify patterns that may require further medical attention.

How It Works

The system is intended as a supportive research and awareness tool to encourage early medical consultation when certain indicators appear. It does not replace professional diagnosis but helps highlight situations where medical evaluation may be beneficial.

Early Detection Importance

Early detection and awareness can play an important role in managing health conditions effectively. This platform aims to provide an accessible and user-friendly way to explore potential early indicators through simple health information. By facilitating awareness and encouraging timely medical consultation, we help support better health outcomes.
